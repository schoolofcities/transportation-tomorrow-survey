These maps were created by Jeff Allen and Muhammad Khalis Bin Samion at the School of Cities, University of Toronto.

All the travel-related data are from the 2022 Transportation Tomorrow Survey. The base-map data pertaining to roads and water are from OpenStreetMap. Cycling data are from Can-BICS (CHATR Lab) and include bike lanes, cycle tracks, and trails. Transit route data are from Metrolinx.

If there's anything on this map you have questions about, have feedback for ways to improve it, or want to see additional data on the map, please add an issue on the GitHub page or just email us directly. Thank you! :)
